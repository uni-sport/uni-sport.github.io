 The focus of our Pilates lessons is on balance and mobility exercises, which are
performed both standing and on the mat. The aim of the course is to develop more
mobility and flexibility in the body. During the lessons, controlled isolation
of certain parts of the body is also practiced, which leads to more mobility of
the entire body. There is something for everyone who wants to be more flexible
in everyday life. The course is also recommended for all dance enthusiasts, as
we also focus on our core, which is important for stability when performing
various dances. Gentle stretching exercises for the inner muscles round off the
profile of the course. Please bring your own mat.
