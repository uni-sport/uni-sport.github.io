 There is no insurance cover for free training! 

 Varied technical and tactical training with a final game form for experienced
handball players. The aim is to promote individual performance and improve
understanding of the game. And of course there is always a good portion of fun
involved. If the weather is good in the summer semester, spontaneous beach
handball sessions are also possible. We look forward to enthusiastic
participants and lots of fun with you in the coming semester. 

 The course is aimed at anyone who wants to gain their first experience in
handball. The course will train the basic technical skills of handball, teamwork
in small groups using handball-specific game forms, and the basics of team
tactics. The main focus, however, should always be on having fun. I'm looking
forward to meeting motivated people and to introducing you to the sport of
handball.
