 The most beautiful sport in the world. The player should have the endurance of a
marathon runner, the speed of a sprinter, the jumping power of a high jumper,
the arm strength of a javelin thrower, the striking power of a blacksmith, the
agility of an artist, the skill of a lacemaker, the reaction speed of a fencer,

cleaner salesman, the frustration tolerance of a dike builder, the mental
toughness of an arctic explorer, the nerves of a demolition expert, the planning
ability of a three-cushion player, the ruthlessness of a colonial master, the
obsession of a mountain climber and the intuition and imagination of an artist.
Because these qualities are so rarely found in one person, there are so few good
badminton players. (Knupp, 1994, p.4) We want to work on this together. The
training is primarily aimed at the needs of badminton beginners. Here, under the
guidance of a competent trainer, you will learn the most important hitting and
running techniques, as well as some tactical basics of the game of badminton.
Please bring your own rackets and balls!
