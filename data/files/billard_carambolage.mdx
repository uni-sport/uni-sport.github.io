<Locale value="en">
 

 Carambola (also called carom), unlike pool billiards or snooker, is only played
with three balls and the aim is to hit the other two balls with the cue ball.
This course is for beginners of all ages, with training for advanced players if
you are interested. You can start at any time. Location: Billardclub Regensburg
1926 e. V, Schottenstr. 4, 93047 Regensburg Contact: Prof. C. Aslanidis, UKR,
Tel.: 0941 944-6206, Email: charalampos.aslanidis@ukr.de
